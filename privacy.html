Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 30, 2025

Overview

Secrets Observatory is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

Information We Collect

Email Subscription

If you choose to subscribe to our email newsletter, we collect:

  • Email address: Used solely to send you information about news and updates and to count subscribers
  • Subscription date: To track when you joined our mailing list

We do not collect:

  • Your name (unless you voluntarily provide it)
  • Any other personal information beyond what's necessary for the subscription (unless you voluntarily provide us with an information relevant for improvment of your experience)

Analytics

We use PostHog, a privacy-focused analytics platform. Our configuration ensures:

  • Cookie consent required: PostHog operates in cookieless mode by default. Cookies are only used if you explicitly accept them via our consent banner
  • Your choice matters: If you reject cookies, we use completely cookieless tracking that cannot identify you across sessions
  • Anonymous data: All analytics data is anonymized and aggregated for product improvement
  • EU data residency: All data is processed and stored on PostHog's EU servers (eu.i.posthog.com)
  • No third-party sharing: Analytics data is never sold or shared with third parties

What We Track

We collect analytics to improve our content and user experience. The analytics include for instance:

  • Page views: Which pages and posts are visited
  • Scroll depth: How far users scroll through posts
  • Time on page: How long users spend reading content
  • Referrer information: Which websites or search engines led you here
  • Browser and device type: General technical information (e.g., "Chrome on Windows", "Safari on iOS")

With cookie consent: We can track you across sessions to understand returning visitors and user journeys. Session replays may be recorded (with all text and inputs masked).

Without cookie consent: Tracking is completely cookieless and cannot identify you across sessions. No session replays are recorded.

Data Retention

  • Analytics data is retained according to PostHog's data retention policies
  • Your cookie consent preference is stored locally in your browser

Your Privacy Rights

  • Control your cookies: Accept or reject cookies via our consent banner
  • Change your mind: Clear your browser's localStorage to see the consent banner again
  • Cookieless by default: If you reject cookies, we use fully anonymous, cookieless tracking

How We Use Your Information

Email Addresses

Your email address is used exclusively to:

  • Send notifications about news and updates
  • Maintain our subscriber list
  • Respond to your inquiries and feedback

We will never:

  • Sell your email address to third parties
  • Share your email with advertisers or marketers
  • Send promotional or commercial emails unrelated to our updates
  • Use your email for any purpose other than our notifications, newsletters and similar

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for email delivery (such as Mailchimp, Buttondown, or similar). These services are bound by their own privacy policies and are required to protect your data. We choose only reputable, privacy-conscious providers.

Data Storage and Security

  • Email addresses are stored securely using industry standards
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data
  • We retain your email only as long as you remain subscribed

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Unsubscribe: You can opt out at any time
  • Access your data: Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Delete your data: Request deletion of your email from our records at any time
  • Update your information: Correct or update your email address

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the Feedback page.

RSS/Atom Feed

Our RSS/Atom feed (feed.xml) does not collect any personal information. Feed readers access the feed directly, and we have no visibility into who subscribes via RSS.

Cookies

This website uses browser storage for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies (No Consent Required)

  • Theme preference: Stores your dark/light mode choice in browser localStorage
  • Cookie consent: Remembers your cookie preference (accept/reject)

Analytics Cookies (Consent Required)

If you accept cookies via our consent banner, PostHog may use cookies to:

  • Track you across sessions to understand user behavior
  • Identify returning visitors
  • Provide more detailed analytics for product improvement
  • Record session replays: Visual recordings of how you interact with our site (with all text and inputs masked for privacy)

Session Replay Privacy: If enabled (only with your consent), session replays are recorded with maximum privacy settings:

  • All text content is masked with asterisks
  • All input fields are masked
  • We can see where you click and navigate, but not what you type or read
  • Replays help us understand usability issues and improve the site experience

If you reject cookies: PostHog operates in cookieless mode using anonymous, server-side tracking that cannot identify you across sessions. No session replays are recorded.

No advertising cookies: We never use advertising or marketing cookies.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the email subscription after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us through the Feedback page.

Legal Basis (GDPR)

For users in the European Union:

  • Email subscription: Based on your explicit consent when you subscribe
  • Analytics: Based on legitimate interest in understanding site usage. We use privacy-preserving, cookieless analytics that does not collect Personal Data as defined by GDPR. The server-side hash used to count visitors is irreversible and cannot be used to identify individuals.
  • Theme preference storage: Based on legitimate interest in providing essential website functionality

Under GDPR Article 6(1)(f), we have a legitimate interest in understanding how our content is used to improve it, provided this is done in a privacy-preserving manner that does not adversely affect your rights. Our cookieless analytics implementation ensures compliance with this principle.

You may withdraw any consent at any time by contacting us via the Feedback page.
