Our Wonder Lab allows curious minds to explore aspects of life they wonder about, with a primary focus on secrets related to light and fluids.

In particular, we focus on understading these two natural phenomena better by as precisely as possible describing and visualising their behaviour. By doing this, we can observe and uncover their characteristics.

To achive the above, we model, simulate and animate both light and fluids. We use mainly two approaches:

  • Starting from a mathematical description of nature to derive the desired natural phenomenon.
  • Observing natural behaviour and subsequently aiming at mathematically describing it.

Finaly, we present all our outcomes (including code) transparent on the webpage, so that anybody can investigate them further. Moreover, in our presentation we try to use ordinary language so that as many people can understand it and explore it for themselves.
This is also one of the reasons we write up the protocols, where anybody can learn how to set up a virtual environment on their computer to try for themselves to model nature :) .
