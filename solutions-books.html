Solutions Library

This page contains solutions to textbooks that do not have any official solutions manual.

Of course, one can find some partial solutions elsewhere on the internet, or one can use AI to supplement these. Yet experience has shown that even if such solutions are provided in one way or another, they usually do not reflect the solution the author of the book clearly had in mind, or are written at an abstraction level/in a language not in accordance with that of the particular book. Moreover, the reader profits most from such solutions, as well as from books, if they are written in a consistent language, such as using the same symbolism and writing style.

This all led to the creation of this solutions library, and the hope is that the reader can thus greatly benefit from such coherent solutions.

The solutions library is currently under construction and so is password protected. You can subscribe to our mailing list if you want to be notified about updates.
